About

Championing Civil Rights with Purpose

Advocate C. Keerthana is a distinguished civil litigation attorney with over eight years of experience navigating the complexities of civil law. Known for a sharp analytical mind and a compassionate approach, she has built a formidable reputation across Tamil Nadu's legal circles.

Her practice is built on a foundation of thorough case preparation, strategic litigation, and an uncompromising commitment to achieving justice for every client she represents — from individuals to corporations.

Recognised for her ability to dissect complex legal issues and present compelling arguments, Advocate Keerthana brings both passion and precision to every courtroom appearance.
